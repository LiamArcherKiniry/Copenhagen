Where to Day-Trip

Louisiana Museum of Modern art

Time to Travel: 45 minutes

Adult Ticket: 130 DKK ~ $20

About:
Just a 45-minute train ride north of Copenhagen, The Louisiana Museum of Modern Art is one of Denmark's premier modern art institutions. Here you can take in the beautiful confluence of art, architecture, and landscape in this seaside estate. With works from hundreds of world renowned artists, a brilliant cafe, and expansive sculpture garden, the Louisiana Museum is a perfect day trip.

Kronborg Castle

Time to Travel:1 hour

Adult Ticket:95 DKK ~ $14

About:
Kronborg Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is said to be the setting of Shakespeare’s Hamlet. It is one of the must-see attractions in Denmark. Kronborg was built between 1574 and 1585 by Frederick II with ornate decoration and imposing architecture in Dutch Renaissance style. The castle is stunning and the surrounding town is a beautiful seaside town with shops as well as a sizeable performance venu.

Malmö, Sweden

Time to Travel:40 minutes

Adult Ticket:N/A

About:
Malmö is Sweden's third largest city. Here you can explore the picturesque old town, relax in the city's many parks, and admire it's blend of architectural styles. Don't be afraid to cross the border into Sweden, just make sure you've got your passport. It's only a 40 minute journey to this lovely city across the Øresund straight.
